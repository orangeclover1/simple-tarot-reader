# Lantern Tarot symbol system

The app uses compact text glyphs instead of card artwork. This keeps it light, fast, accessible, and legally uncomplicated.

## Major Arcana

Each Major Arcana card has one minimal abstract glyph, such as `○` for The Fool, `✦` for The Magician, `☾` for The High Priestess, and `ϟ` for The Tower.

## Minor Arcana

Minor cards combine a suit glyph and rank:

- Wands: `│`
- Cups: `∪`
- Swords: `†`
- Pentacles: `●`

Examples: `│A` is Ace of Wands, `∪Q` is Queen of Cups, `†10` is Ten of Swords, and `●K` is King of Pentacles.

These can later be replaced by custom SVG line icons while preserving the same data field (`glyph`).
