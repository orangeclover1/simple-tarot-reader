## Full description

Lantern Tarot is a private, lightweight tarot journal designed for thoughtful reflection.

Choose from simple one-card guidance, three-card readings, relationship spreads, the Celtic Cross, and more. Explore detailed upright and reversed meanings, symbolism, reflective yes/no guidance, elements, astrology, and journaling prompts for all 78 cards.

Save how each reading made you feel, add private notes, and notice patterns through local insight charts showing frequent cards, suits, orientations, feelings, and reading activity.

Features:

- Complete 78-card tarot reference
- Multiple tarot spreads
- Upright and reversed meanings
- Symbolism, elements, astrology, and reflection prompts
- Yes, no, and maybe guidance
- Feeling tags and free-text journaling
- Frequent-pull and reading-pattern insights
- Multiple pixel-art themes and card backs
- Works fully offline
- No account required
- No advertisements or tracking

Your readings and notes remain stored locally on your device.

Lantern Tarot is intended for reflection and entertainment and is not a replacement for professional medical, legal, financial, or mental-health advice.
