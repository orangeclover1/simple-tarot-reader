# Lantern Tarot Privacy Policy

**Last updated: [DATE]**

Lantern Tarot is designed as an offline tarot reading and journaling application.

## Information the app handles

The app may store information that you enter or generate, including tarot readings, questions, feeling labels, and journal notes. This information is stored locally on your device.

## Data collection and sharing

Lantern Tarot does not transmit your readings or journal content to the developer. The current version does not include advertising, analytics, account registration, cloud synchronization, or third-party tracking.

## Permissions

The current version does not request internet, location, contacts, camera, microphone, or other sensitive device permissions.

## Data deletion

You can remove locally stored data by deleting readings within the app where supported, clearing the app's storage through Android settings, or uninstalling the app. Uninstalling the app may permanently remove its locally stored data.

## Children

Lantern Tarot is intended as a general reflection and entertainment tool and is not specifically directed to children.

## Disclaimer

Tarot readings are provided for reflection and entertainment. They are not a substitute for medical, legal, financial, or mental-health advice.
